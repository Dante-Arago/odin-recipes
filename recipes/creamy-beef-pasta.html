Creamy Beef Pasta Recipe

This one pot creamy beef pasta is the perfect no-fuss hearty dinner!

This is one of those dinners that you can always rely on for a simple midweek dinner. It uses a simple selection of ingredients but it is truly delicious! Follow me

Ingredients List:

Seasoning Mix

  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 3/4 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/4 to 1/2 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 1/4 tsp Dried Thyme

Pasta

  • 1 medium White Onion
  • 3 cloves of Garlic
  • 500g / 1lb Minced/Ground Beef
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 600ml / 2 1/2 cups Beef Stock
  • 500g / 2 cups Tomato Passata
  • 180ml / 3/4 cup Double/Heavy Cream
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 300g / 10.5oz uncooked Conchiglie Pasta
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Add a drizzle of oil to a large pot over medium heat and fry the onion until it begins to soften and turn golden. Add the garlic and fry for a minute longer, then add in the beef. Break it up with your wooden spoon until browned.
  2. Stir in the seasoning mix, then stir in the tomato puree and fry for a minute or so. Stir in the stock, passata (swill out with a splash of water), cream and Worcestershire sauce, then stir in the uncooked pasta.
  3. Bring to a boil and cook until the sauce has thickened and the pasta is al dente, stirring fairly frequently. If the sauce reduces and thickens up before the pasta is cooked through, just add in a splash of hot water as needed to cook the pasta through.
  4. Check for seasoning and adjust with salt, pepper and chilli flakes, then serve up with fresh parsley and enjoy!
